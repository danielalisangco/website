Individual Film:

Faith
Director


My project focuses on the topic of religion and one's devotion, technically still a Catholic, I have chosen my own religion to craft a film on and outline some of the positives and negatives of following on such religion. All I aimed to do was to explore this theme with the least amount of bias possible, by making it a double perspective, with each character meeting their respective conclusion that helps them grow into a better person.

Obviously, with my limited resources and time, as well as the time-limit being under three minutes, I am unable to fully explore in-depth the behemoth that is Religion. Furthermore, it is a shame that the camera settings were accidentally changed for at least one quarter of the entire film, severely downgrading the experience of those scenes.

Overall, I am somewhat satisfied with the end result or at least the script as I am sure that I have been able to get the point across. Of course, I am a bit disappointed in the quality of some of the shots, and lighting could have been reworked, especially in the beach scenes.

If I were to remake the film, I would also definitely look over my script more, storyboard and get a better idea of what I want the characters to sound like. I will also take into account how I would like to edit it post-production and cater my script and filming skills to connect with it. Furthermore, looking back on my first submission, it appears I did not include the Director's Statement that I had produced, so double-checking my submissions will be another aspect that I would note.
