Since the Reds

Since the Reds

2nd June 2024

NOTE: Information is as of mid-2024 and highly simplified due to time constrictions.

Two-minute summary of the Russian Federation from the end of the Soviet Union as of 2024.

Synopsis:

As the largest nation in terms of land size, Russia plays a significant role in international geopolitics. Into the Red is a two minute information video that oversimplies Russia's transition from Gorbachev's reign in 1991 up until the video's conception on May 2024.

It briefly details key events such as the fall of the USSR, Putin's rise to power, the Chechen Wars and the Russo-Ukrainian war.
